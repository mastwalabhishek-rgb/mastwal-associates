Project Overview

Living Around a Courtyard

The Urban Courtyard Home is designed around a central open space that brings natural light and ventilation into every room of the house.

Located in the spiritual city of Rishikesh, this home blends traditional courtyard living with modern amenities, creating a peaceful sanctuary in an urban setting.

Design Concept

The Heart of the Home

The central courtyard acts as the heart of the home, around which all living spaces are organized. This traditional concept has been reimagined for contemporary living, providing natural cooling and a private outdoor space.

The design responds to Rishikesh's warm climate by maximizing shade and cross-ventilation, reducing the need for artificial cooling and creating a sustainable living environment.
