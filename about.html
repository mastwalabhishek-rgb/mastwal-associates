Our Story

Architecture Studio Based in Dehradun

Mastwal Associates is an architecture studio focused on creating meaningful residential and commercial spaces. We believe that thoughtful design has the power to transform how people live, work, and experience their environment.

Founded with a passion for climate-responsive and context-driven architecture, we work closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and translate them into functional, beautiful spaces that stand the test of time.

Meet The Founder

Passionate About Design Excellence

With a deep understanding of spatial design and construction, our founder brings years of experience in creating residential and commercial environments that respond thoughtfully to context, climate, and user needs.

Every project at Mastwal Associates begins with listening — understanding the client's vision, analyzing the site conditions, and developing solutions that balance aesthetics with functionality.

— Mastwal Associates
