Project Overview

A Home Designed for Living

The Hillview Residence is organized around natural light and cross ventilation, responding to the sloping terrain and climatic conditions of Dehradun.

Our approach focused on creating a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor spaces, while maintaining privacy and maximizing views of the surrounding hills.

Design Concept

Blending with Nature

The residence is designed to blend harmoniously with its natural surroundings. Large windows frame views of the hills, while carefully positioned openings ensure optimal cross-ventilation.

The layout separates private and communal zones while maintaining visual continuity. Circulation flows naturally from entry to living spaces, ensuring spatial efficiency and comfort.
